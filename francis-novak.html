Francis Novak

Francis Novak is a portrait and fashion photographer. He made a breakthrough in the sphere of fashion photography with his exceptional portraits of Madonna, Kate Moss and the members of the Royal Family. In his portraits, Francis Novak recreates certain day-to-day scenes while involving big groups of people in order to make the shot as realistic as possible.
