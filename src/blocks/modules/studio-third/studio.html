Studio 3

Studio 3 is our total blackout studio. A total space of 750sq ft. with 3.5m ceilings.

This blackout studio can be joined with Studio 4 to create a 1600sq ft studio.

The studio is open plan. There is a hair & makeup station, sofa and table (these can all be removed if you need an empty space).

The studio is painted totally black. Walls, ceiling and floor are black. The studio has a double door loading bay and it's on the ground floor for easy loading and access.

This blackout photography and filming studio is ideal for music videos and photography where you need to control the light.
