Studio 1

Studio 1 is one of our biggest studios. 1300sq ft of open space with 3.5m ceiling.

Studio 1 is open plan and can be arranged as per your requirements. The Studio is very well equipped with everything you might need for a production. The photography and filming studio is on the first floor and it has a goods lift.

This Studio is a full daylight studio with large east and west facing windows. The Studio can also be completely blacked out.

This Photography & Video Studio in London has large Make up/Dressing area, a lounge area with Chesterfield sofa, armchair and original brickwork wall that can also be used as a backdrop.

This studio has a large fully fitted and modern kitchen for food shoots or food preparation.
